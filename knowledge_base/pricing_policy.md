# Pricing Policy

## Overview
Our platform offers three pricing tiers designed to serve startups, growing businesses, and enterprise organizations. All plans include core CRM features, email integration, and standard support.

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter Plan
- **Price:** $49/month (billed monthly) or $39/month (billed annually)
- **Seats:** Up to 5 users
- **API Calls:** 100 requests/minute
- **Storage:** 10GB
- **Support:** Email support, 48-hour response SLA
- **Best for:** Freelancers, solo founders, very small teams

### Standard Plan
- **Price:** $149/month (billed monthly) or $119/month (billed annually)
- **Seats:** Up to 20 users
- **API Calls:** 1,000 requests/minute
- **Storage:** 100GB
- **Support:** Email + chat support, 24-hour response SLA
- **Best for:** Small to mid-size businesses, growing teams

### Professional Plan
- **Price:** $299/month (billed monthly) or $239/month (billed annually)
- **Seats:** Up to 50 users
- **API Calls:** 5,000 requests/minute
- **Storage:** 500GB
- **Support:** Priority support, 4-hour response SLA
- **Best for:** Scaling companies with high email volume

### Enterprise Plan
- **Price:** Custom pricing (contact sales)
- **Seats:** Unlimited
- **API Calls:** Custom limits (up to 50,000 requests/minute)
- **Storage:** Custom
- **Support:** Dedicated account manager, 1-hour response SLA, 24/7 phone support
- **Best for:** Large organizations, regulated industries, multi-region deployments
- **Includes:** Custom SLA, HIPAA BAA, GDPR DPA, SOC 2 audit reports, SSO, custom data residency

## Pro-Rata Billing Rules

When a customer upgrades or adds seats mid-billing cycle, pro-rata billing applies.

### How Pro-Rata is Calculated
- **Formula:** (Days remaining in cycle / Total days in cycle) × Price difference
- Charges are applied immediately to the card on file
- The new plan takes effect immediately upon upgrade

### Example Calculation
- Customer on Standard plan ($149/month), upgrades to Professional ($299/month) on day 15 of a 30-day cycle
- Days remaining: 15
- Price difference per month: $150
- Pro-rata charge: (15/30) × $150 = **$75.00**
- Next full billing: $299/month

### Adding Seats Mid-Cycle
- Additional seats are charged pro-rata for the remainder of the current billing cycle
- Formula: (Days remaining / Total days) × (Per-seat price)
- Standard plan per-seat price beyond included seats: $12/seat/month
- Professional plan per-seat price beyond included seats: $8/seat/month

### Example: Adding 5 Seats Mid-Cycle
- Customer on Standard plan, adding 5 seats on day 10 of a 30-day cycle
- Days remaining: 20
- Per-seat monthly cost: $12
- Pro-rata charge: (20/30) × (5 × $12) = (0.667) × $60 = **$40.00**
- From next cycle: base plan + 5 additional seats = $149 + $60 = $209/month

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I change plans at any time?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with pro-rata billing. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**What happens if I exceed my seat limit?**
You will be prompted to upgrade. New users cannot be added until the plan is upgraded or existing users are removed.

**Do you offer a free trial?**
Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Trial accounts have Standard plan limits.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and ACH bank transfer (Enterprise only).

**Are there setup fees?**
No setup fees on Starter, Standard, or Professional plans. Enterprise onboarding fee may apply depending on customization requirements.
